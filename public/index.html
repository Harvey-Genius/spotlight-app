Install in 3 steps

Takes about 30 seconds. No store needed.

1

Download & unzip

Click the download button above. Unzip the file — you'll get a folder with the extension files.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Go to chrome://extensions in your browser. Enable Developer mode (toggle in the top right).

3

Load the extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the unzipped folder. Spotlight will appear in your extensions bar — click it to open the side panel.
